Approach -

At HRT Consulting, we deeply understand our clients' needs to set clear transformation goals. Through collaborative discussions and detailed analysis, we create a strategic roadmap, prioritizing initiatives for maximum impact and feasibility. Our tailored change management plans ensure smooth implementation and stakeholder buy-in. We provide customized training for digital adaptation and focus on governance, measurement, and monitoring to track progress effectively. By analyzing HR processes, systems, and culture, we identify gaps and develop strategies to leverage strengths and mitigate risks. Dedicated to guiding clients through every transformation stage, we ensure they achieve their desired outcomes and thrive in today's dynamic business landscape. Let's embark on this exciting journey together.

HR Strategy and Setup
Our comprehensive HR services align with your organizational goals by developing HR strategies, designing structures, drafting policies, and ensuring legal compliance. We offer recruitment and training services, standardize key processes, and enhance recruitment efforts to attract, develop, and retain top talent.
HR Transformation
Our HR services position HR as a strategic partner, driving organizational change and decision-making through upskilling, digital capabilities, and people analytics. We generate data-driven insights to influence business outcomes and enhance HR effectiveness.
HR Process Optimization
Our HR services include process mapping to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies, followed by collaborative redesign to streamline workflows. We enhance efficiency by automating tasks and reorganizing responsibilities.
Rewards Setup and Management
We benchmark compensation and benefits to ensure competitiveness and provide insights on industry trends, offering strategic recommendations through outsourced salary reviews. Our services include designing variable pay programs, implementing deferred reward plans, and integrating compensation strategies with career planning and international mobility analysis.
Talent Acquisition
Our executive search services employ thorough processes to find and attract top leadership talent, offering insights on market trends and competitor intelligence. We ensure successful integration and retention through customized onboarding, ongoing support, and robust assessment methods, including competency-based interviews and psychometric evaluations.
CHRO-As-A-Service
We conduct strategic organizational reviews to enhance performance and align operations with business goals. Our services include optimizing structures, implementing best-in-class HR processes, developing HR teams, and ensuring HR representation in management forums for optimal support and success.
